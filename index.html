Strategy

Strategy is not operational excellence, it is positioning your company for success. We can help you do this using design thinking methodologies to really understand your business and then enable a series of internal and external innovations to let you take your business to the next level.

Innovation

Being innovative is not a single step; it is about creating a culture that is accepting of change and failure. This can only be done with trust. We can help run workshops and develop communication plans the enable this trust and help you to understand and build your company culture.

We take our inspiration from the continual rebirth of the mythical phoenix combined with the Nordic ideals of how to collaborate.
